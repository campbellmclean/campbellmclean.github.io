Video guidelines

In a nutshell, we want each video to be no longer than 5-7 min. We would prefer you to create a series of videos rather than one big lesson video. There is no need to add in intro/outros or graphics, we do this for you.

Camera

  • Use a high-definition (HD) camera or a modern smartphone with good video capabilities (1080p or 4K resolution).
  • If possible, use a camera with manual settings for exposure, focus, and white balance for more control over the video quality.

Lighting

  • Good lighting is essential for clear and well-lit videos.
  • Utilize natural light from windows whenever possible, or invest in softbox or LED lights to create even and flattering illumination.
  • Avoid harsh shadows and ensure that your face and surroundings are well-lit.

Audio

  • High-quality audio is equally important as video quality.
  • Ideally, use an external microphone, such as a lavalier or shotgun microphone, to capture clear and crisp audio.
  • Minimize background noise by recording in a quiet environment or using soundproofing materials.

Background

  • Choose a clean and uncluttered background that is relevant to the content.
  • A neutral or professional setting is often preferred for learning videos.
  • Consider using a green screen for more versatility in creating custom backgrounds.

Tripod or Stabilization

  • Use a tripod or other stabilization equipment to avoid shaky footage.
  • Steady shots contribute to a more polished and professional look.

Framing and Composition

  • Frame yourself in the center or off-center, following the rule of thirds.
  • Ensure there is enough headroom and space around you, but not too much empty space.
  • Pay attention to your positioning and gestures to maintain a visually engaging presentation

Script or Outline

  • Prepare a script or detailed outline before recording to stay focused and organized.
  • Practice your delivery to sound natural and confident.

Editing

Edit your videos to remove mistakes, pauses, and any irrelevant content.

Export and Compression

  • Videos should not exceed 200MB. You may need to compress them to meet this requirement. There are many free tools available to achieve this.
  • We support the following formats: MP4, MOV, WMV, AVI, and FLV
