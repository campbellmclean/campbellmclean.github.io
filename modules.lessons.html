Adding Lessons

The learning content can be organized into sections and lessons. The sections help break up groups of lessons into logical blocks.

For example, a module structure might look something like this

  1. Introduction to Inclusion in the Workplace
    • What is Inclusion?
    • Importance of Inclusive Workplaces
    • Benefits of Embracing Diversity and Inclusion
  2. Understanding Diversity in the Workplace
    • Definition of Diversity and Its Dimensions
    • Recognizing and Valuing Individual Differences
    • Overcoming Biases and Stereotypes
  3. Creating an Inclusive Culture
    • Leadership's Role in Fostering Inclusivity
    • Establishing Inclusive Policies and Practices
    • Building Diverse and Inclusive Teams
  4. Conclusion
    • Recap of Key Learnings on Inclusion in the Workplace
    • Commitment to Championing Inclusion in Professional Settings
    • Empowering Positive Change through Inclusive Leadership

Media

The lesson type is either video or document.

Lesson type - Video

Your lesson content in video form, ideally no longer than 5-7 min. See our guidelines for creating video content.

Lesson type - Document

A lesson presented in document form.

  • Text: A plain .txt file
  • PDF: Standard .pdf document
  • Document: In .doc format, could be from Google Docs or Microsoft.
  • Download: Any file which will be available as a download
