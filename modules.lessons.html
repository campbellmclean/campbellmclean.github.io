Lessons

Several Lessons combine together to make up a Section. These can be smaller video segments, documents, references or other useful resources

Example

For example, a module learning content structure might look something like this:

  1. Introduction to Inclusion in the Workplace
    • What is Inclusion? lesson
    • Importance of Inclusive Workplaces lesson
    • Benefits of Embracing Diversity and Inclusion lesson

Here there are 3 lessons which make up the section "Introduction to Inclusion in the Workplace".

The lesson type is either video or document.

Lesson type - Video

Your lesson content in video form, ideally no longer than 5-7 min. See our guidelines for creating video content.

Lesson type - Document

A lesson presented in document form.

  • Text: A plain .txt file
  • PDF: Standard .pdf document
  • Document: In .doc format, could be from Google Docs or Microsoft.
  • Download: Any file which will be available as a download
