Sections

The learning content can be organized into sections and lessons. The sections help break up groups of lessons into logical blocks, like the chapters of a book.

Example

For example, a module learning content structure might look something like this:

  1. Introduction to Inclusion in the Workplace section
    • What is Inclusion?
    • Importance of Inclusive Workplaces
    • Benefits of Embracing Diversity and Inclusion
  2. Understanding Diversity in the Workplace section
    • Definition of Diversity and Its Dimensions
    • Recognizing and Valuing Individual Differences
    • Overcoming Biases and Stereotypes
  3. Creating an Inclusive Culture section
    • Leadership's Role in Fostering Inclusivity
    • Establishing Inclusive Policies and Practices
    • Building Diverse and Inclusive Teams
  4. Conclusion section
    • Recap of Key Learnings on Inclusion in the Workplace
    • Commitment to Championing Inclusion in Professional Settings
    • Empowering Positive Change through Inclusive Leadership

There are 4 logical sections breaking up the content.

Name

Create a title for the section which is accurate and concise.
